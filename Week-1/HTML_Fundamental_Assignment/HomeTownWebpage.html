Muzaffarpur, Bihar

Location

Around 80km away from Patna. You can reach this place via rail, road, and air from any part of India. It has two airports located at Patna & Dharbanga, and 2 major railway station at Muzaffarpur and Patna

About

Litchi

Muzaffarpur is a very lively place with lot of lively people to meet. It s most popular for it iconic exotic fruit Sahi Litchi

Environment cleanliness

Generally when you travel in other cities,, you may find pollution containing factory harmful particles, but here when you travel there is no such harmful pollution, and max pollution that you will get will be normal soil dust

Remaining Gem Info.

Apart from litch, the place is also popular for special bangles called Lehthi, ,the people of here are very sweet, humble and fantastic. ,The greenary and factory free environment makes you forgot the city hustle and bustle, The town also has orient club, chakkar ground along with many historic places to visit.LS college is one of the historic college where our first president Rajendra Prasad taught The place was earlier known as Tirhut. There many other things to tell, that will make you connect your soul with nature, history & understand the importance of the place. But that was the short overview

Weather

The place is blessed with all kind of human friendly weathers.Though sometimes it gets extreme in winter and summer, else the mild cold winds along with view of nature, makes you fall in love with environment

Population

The population is around 15-20 lacs

Sport

Cricket, football, Kabaddi, Batminton,are popular sports here
